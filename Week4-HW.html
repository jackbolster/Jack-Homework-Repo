About TacoTurtle

TacoTurtle

TacoTurtle is a YouTube channel I started when I was twelve years old. I had a short but good run until I got very sick and had to quit for my own health. Now I'm back into it and post only animations as of now, but I hope to grow and start posting different content such as:

  • Streams
  • Updates
  • Actual consistency

How Codecademy is involved

TacoTurtle is a reason that I am excited about codecademy, as I believe that knowledge of JavaScript, HTML, and CSS will be a major factor in creating content faster and better. I'm trying to upload an episode per month of an animated elimination-based competition show called Inanimate Island, so check it out if that sounds interesting to you. Some things I hope to achieve as a result of codecademy are:

  • automated animation from a custom database
  • automated lip syncing
  • a manual interface with a collection of pre-animated shots of characters
